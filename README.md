# Hotel Booking Cancellation & Dynamic Pricing Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Hotel Booking Cancellation & Dynamic Pricing Analysis** project aims to analyze hotel booking data to identify factors influencing booking cancellations and seasonal pricing trends.

The project focuses on customer retention strategies and revenue optimization by leveraging **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**, **Data Visualization**, **Machine Learning**, and **Business Intelligence** techniques.

### Objectives

* Identify key factors driving booking cancellations.
* Understand customer booking behavior.
* Analyze seasonal demand and pricing trends.
* Support dynamic pricing strategies.
* Build predictive models for booking cancellation.
* Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Customers with a history of previous cancellations are significantly more likely to cancel future bookings.
* Returning customers with successful booking history show much lower cancellation rates.
* Non-refundable deposits have the highest cancellation rates among all deposit types.
* Longer lead times are associated with higher cancellation probability.
* Average Daily Rate (ADR) increases during peak travel seasons, reaching its highest value in August.
* Booking cancellations exhibit clear seasonal patterns throughout the year.
